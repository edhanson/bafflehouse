BAFFLEHOUSE — HOW TO PLAY
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Bafflehouse is a classic interactive fiction adventure game. The idea is 
that the player navigates through a virtual world based on detailed text
descriptions of the surroundings by issuing text commands. There are a few
commands that can be executed via a single word or character, but most
commands are of the form of a verb followed by a noun. However, the parser
is capable of interpreting many idiomatic variations of the available
commands, as well as adjectives, conjunctions, and prepositions to 
encourage ineractions using more natural language.

MOVEMENT
  The parser understands 6 key directions (north, south, east, west, up, and 
  down). To move between rooms, you can enter commands such as "go north", 
  but you can also use single-letter shorthand directions like "n", "s", "e", 
  "w", "u", and "d". Other commands may be interpreted by the parser as in a 
  particular direction. 

LOOKING AROUND
  The following commands are useful to orient oneself within the game world.

  look                                  Describe the current room.
  examine <thing>                       Inspect something closely.
  inventory                             List what you are carrying.

TAKING AND DROPPING
  Collecting useful items is key to progressing in your journey.

  take <thing>                          Pick something up.
  drop <thing>                          Put something down.
  put <thing> in <container>            Place an item inside something.

DOORS AND LOCKS
  You will need to interact with doors and keys.

  open <door>                           Open a door or container.
  close <door>                          Close it.
  unlock <door> with <key>              Unlock a locked door or container.
  lock <door> with <key>                Lock it again.

OBJECTS AND PUZZLES
  There are various puzzles that require interacting with items and features
  of rooms. Here are some sample commands that you may find handy.

  read <thing>                          Read a readable item.
  pull <thing>                          Pull something (levers, handles).
  push <thing>                          Push something.
  light <thing>                         Light a lamp or candle.
  extinguish <thing>                    Put out a flame.
  fill <container> with <liquid>        Pour liquid into a container.
  pour <liquid> into <container>        The same, phrased differently.
  wear <item>                           Put on armor or accessories.
  remove <item>                         Take off armor or accessories.
  wield <weapon>                        Ready a weapon.
  unmount <item>                        Take a mounted item off a wall.

NATURAL LANGUAGE
  The parser understands many phrasings beyond the examples above. 
  "I want to take the key", "pick it up", "look at the portraits",
  "try the brass key on the lock", "give the cat some food" — all work.
  When a command is ambiguous, the game will ask you to clarify. If things
  happens, you must rewrite your command with the added detail.

THE CAT
  A grey cat lives in the manor. It is cautious at first.
  Spend time near it, offer it food, and be patient.

COMBAT
  A hostile entity lurks in the Bafflehouse. Arm yourself with armor and
  weapons in you case you get ambushed. If you do enter into combat, the 
  game will enter a special combat mode with specific options outlined.

SCORING
  Certain events may earn you points. A special message will display
  whenever this happens.

TURNS
  The game will track the number of turns that have elapsed. Not every 
  action will advance the turn clock, nor will misunderstood commands.

TIPS
  — Read things you find. Notes and journals contain clues.
  — Examine everything, including scenery mentioned in descriptions.
  — If something seems interactive, try pulling or pushing it.
  — "You notice nothing special about it" means it is decorative.
  — "You don't see that here" means it is not in this room.

OTHER COMMANDS
  quit                                  End the session.
  exit                                  End the session.
  save                                  Save your game progress.
  help                                  Show this message.
  status                                Display your character's status.
